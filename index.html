Process

How the Repository Stays Living

Anyone with knowledge of their country's latest policy can contribute in under 10 minutes. No barriers, anonymous submission supported.

01

Submit via Form

Use the Google Form to submit or update your country's NSP or NHP — PDF upload or URL link. Anonymous submission available for contributors in sensitive roles.

02

Regional Review

Our global team (2–3 reviewers per WHO region) validates submissions and assigns status categories from the manuscript within 20 days.

03

Map Updates

Verified documents are added to the repository and the interactive map reflects updated status during our fortnightly review cycle, tapering to monthly in sustainment phase.

04

Join the Network

Contributors can opt in to our Global NSP Hub-and-Spokes Network and stay connected with surgical policy champions across all WHO regions.
